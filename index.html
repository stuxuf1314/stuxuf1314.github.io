About Me

Who I am

I am a PhD student in the TEA team of the Inria Rennes - Bretagne Atlantique research centre.
I'm interested in:

  • Software Modeling. e.g. Synchronous Language Modeling
  • Formal Verification. e.g. Theorem Proving (Coq, F*)
  • Formal Languages Theory. e.g. Formal Semantics, Hoare Logic
Recently, I am doing the formal verification of RIOT OS's subsystems in Coq & F*, a part of the riot-fp project: proving their safety & security properties!
